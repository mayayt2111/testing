IT Innovation Inc. Visit

We visited IT Innovation Inc. A IT company in Japn which provides consulting and project management services and has helped as many as 300 companies reform themselves.

Our visit to IT Innovation Inc. is made possible thanks to Ms. Christine Yau, Mr. Takeshi Hayama, and Mr. Tomohiro Okuda.

Reached the office by 16:30 as planned.

Presentation Delivered by Professionals

We could sense their comfortable working environment, as soon as we walked through the door. After a quick introduction, we had the privilege of a presentation session where we learned from top-notch developers and project managers. They shared information related to Green Transformation, IT Architecture, and AI Solutions, which broadened our horizons. To express our gratitude, we specially prepared some small gifts for them. We were delighted that they very much enjoyed these souvenirs.

Student Discussion

After the presentation, we each shared the points that left the deepest impression on us. This allowed us to further explore the topics.

Overall, this visit was fruitful. It not only allowed us to experience the work atmosphere of the IT company but also expanded our knowledge. This visit experience will undoubtedly have a positive impact on our future learning and work.
